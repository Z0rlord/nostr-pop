# Yawara (柔)

## Περίληψη

 *(Αυτόματη περίληψη· δείτε τα αγγλικά για λεπτομέρειες.)*

**Unarmed grappling and restraint** — called **Yawara** (柔) on the international site and **jujutsu** (柔術) in Japanese official material. One of eight major arts in Tenshinryu hyoho (see [[arts/_index]]).

This wiki uses slug **yawara** to match the international eight-art list; content covers the same unarmed line.
