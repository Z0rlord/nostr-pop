# Tesajutsu (鉄鎖術)

## Περίληψη

 *(Αυτόματη περίληψη· δείτε τα αγγλικά για λεπτομέρειες.)*

The art of the **iron chain** (*tesa*, 鉄鎖) — flexible metal links used to strike, entangle, or disarm. One of eight arts named on the international Tenshinryu site (see [[arts/_index]]), distinct from [[arts/kusarigamajutsu|kusarigamajutsu]] (chain **and** sickle).

Published site material lists tesajutsu alongside kusarigama, spear, and glaive arts as components of **comprehensive hyoho**. Detailed kata names and progression are not yet published in this wiki's ingested sources; instruction belongs to licensed teachers.
