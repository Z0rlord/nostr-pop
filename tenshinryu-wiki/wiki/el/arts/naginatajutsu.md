# Naginatajutsu (薙刀術)

## Περίληψη

 *(Αυτόματη περίληψη· δείτε τα αγγλικά για λεπτομέρειες.)*

The art of the **glaive** (*naginata*) — a curved blade on a long shaft. One of eight major arts in Tenshinryu hyoho (see [[arts/_index]]), grouped with other **long weapons** under Hozoin-line spear tradition.

The official site notes that **sojutsu**, **jumonjiyarijutsu**, and **naginatajutsu** derive from **Hozoin-ryu In-ha** (宝蔵院分流陰派槍術). Naginata methods are preserved inside comprehensive hyoho rather than as a standalone naginata school.
