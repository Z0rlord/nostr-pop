# Kusarigamajutsu (鎖鎌術)

## Περίληψη

 *(Αυτόματη περίληψη· δείτε τα αγγλικά για λεπτομέρειες.)*

The art of the **chain-sickle** (*kusarigama*) — a weighted chain attached to a hand-held sickle or blade. Listed among eight major arts on the international site; in Japanese curricular terms it is **tonomono** (外物, supplementary transmission) alongside other non-core weapons (see [[arts/_index]]).

Kusarigama belongs to the category of **irregular or specialized weapons** (*henzoku buki*, 変則武器) that Edo-period warriors might encounter. Tenshinryu's comprehensive philosophy holds that sword skill alone is insufficient for every battlefield or street encounter — chain weapons, spears, and unarmed methods fill the gap (see [[philosophy/kata-culture]]).
