# Guidelines for Teaching Beginners

## Περίληψη

Παιδαγωγικό κείμενο του **Kuwami Masakumo** (10ος Shike) για εκπαιδευτές Tenshinryu Hyōhō.

## Βασικές προϋποθέσεις

- Weave in terminology naturally (e.g. **koiguchi** 鯉口 when teaching *Sōsen*).
- Keep advanced students engaged: questions, eye contact, storytelling.
- Lower bar + praise for coordination struggles
- Higher bar for prior experience
- [[techniques/tachiai-12-kata]] — advanced curriculum (not beginner material)
- [[concepts/seiho]] — special kata category; textbooks assume prior instruction
