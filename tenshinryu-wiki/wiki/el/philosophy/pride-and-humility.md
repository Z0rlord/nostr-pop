# Hold pride that is not arrogance,and humility that is not self-deprecation (驕りと謙虚)
## Περίληψη

 *(Αυτόματη περίληψη· δείτε τα αγγλικά για λεπτομέρειες.)*

Hold pride that is not arrogance,
and humility that is not self-deprecation
奢りではない自信と、卑屈ではない謙虚さ
Ogoridehanaijishinto,Hikutudehanaikenkyosa
This is what is taught as the most important principle in Tenshin-ryu.
When this balance is lost, one strays from the path of being human and becomes trapped in life.
