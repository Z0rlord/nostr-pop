# Shin-no-Kurai (真の位)

**Chudan (中段)** — fist at torso height. Core defensive and transitional postures.
| Kurai | Notes |
|-------|--------|
| **Seigan-no-Kurai** 青眼 | Tsukagashira at **suigetsu**; kissaki between enemy eyes; highly defensive. Kanji variants: 静眼 (observe quietly) vs 勢眼 (ready intensity) |
| **Katame Hazushi Seigan** 片目外しの青眼 | Tip to left eye; fists left hip; **Sasoigaeshi** feint |
| **Tate-no-Kurai** 楯 (Shin) | Shield at chest; **Chuho / Nakamin** 中峯 — low mountain; impregnable yet mobile |
| **Jyatai-no-Kurai** 蛇躰 (Shin) | Snake; wrists at chest; less forward lean than Ten version |
| **Nokiha Nozoki** 軒端覗 | Peeking from eaves |
| **Katagake-no-Kurai** 肩掛 | Sword on shoulder; **zanshin**; after kesahazushi |
| **Kisodezuka** 亀袖柄 | Turtle in sleeve; elbows on ribs; maai control |
| **Kaedegakure-no-Kurai** 楓隠 | Maple palm invite from Suigetsu line |
| **Yokobue-no-Tachi** (Shin) | Lower tip; waiting; defends Ten/Shin/Chi attacks |
| **Yunzei Shishi-no-Mai** 弓勢獅子舞 | Bow tension + lion dance; **Nukarumi** / mud; Kyokuboku; Koden armor |
