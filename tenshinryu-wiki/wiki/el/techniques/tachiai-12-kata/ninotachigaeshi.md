# 10 — Ninotachigaeshi (二ノ太刀返)
## Περίληψη

 *(Αυτόματη περίληψη· δείτε τα αγγλικά για λεπτομέρειες.)*

Standing/walking; enemy one-hand cut at **leg/shin**.

1. Draw; left foot left; **right knee raised** — one-leg stance; tip down (十字受 cross block)
2. Enemy stops shin cut; cuts to head — **kabuse-uke** (covering block), tip left to top
3. Right foot down; left knee to ground; **Tōrōgaeshi**-like upward cut to forearm (left hand on mine)
4. Step left forward; cut straight down
5. Kumoi → Sekiun → Seigan → noto (method free)
