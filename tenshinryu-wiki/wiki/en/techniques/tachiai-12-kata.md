# Tachiai Battojutsu — 12 Seiho (勢法)
![12 Kata](/assets/promo/12%20kata%20promo.jpg)

Advanced standing draw-and-cut curriculum of [[arts/battojutsu]]. Authored overview by **10th Shike Kuwami Masakumo** (copyright 2020). Confidential teaching (門外不出).

See [[concepts/seiho]] for terminology. Japanese textbook adds **術歌** (*jutka*) for several techniques.

## Common finishing pattern

Many seiho end with retreat ≥ 180 cm (六尺以上) → **Kumoinokurai** (雲居の位) → **Sekiunnokurai** (切雲の位) → **Zanshin** → **Seigannokurai** (青眼の位) → **Zanshin** → **Noto** (method often unspecified).

See [[concepts/miden-kurai-no-koto]] for full kurai definitions.

Some use **Ogesanokurai** (大袈裟の位) / **Hassonokurai** (八草の位) instead. Seiho **6–9** specify **Tenkachiburui** (転柄血振) and **Sakatenoto** (逆手納刀).

## Training progression notes

- **Nukiai #2** was historically taught **first** because Omokage was considered too difficult (JP text).
- Stages often progress: static → walking → running; with optional **Hisen** (飛扇 — fan throw) as feint before draw.
- Footwork variants: **Shumokuashi** (撞木足), **Yagyugoshi** (柳生腰), **Torabashiri** (虎走).

## Pedagogy
