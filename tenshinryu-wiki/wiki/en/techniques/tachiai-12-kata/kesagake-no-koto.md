# 12 — Kesagake-no-koto (袈裟懸ノ事)

![Seiho 12: Kesagake-no-koto 袈裟懸ノ事](/assets/tachiai-12-kata/kesagake-no-koto/%E9%98%B2%E5%88%80/DSC_0386.JPG)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hidden name** | Raiun 霆雲 |
| **Type** | Attack + defense |

## Attack (攻)

From **Yagyugoshi**, **Torabashiri** toward enemy; throw fan; **in-yo** turn, draw, **kesagake**.

## Defense (防)

Enemy cuts straight down at head — you **kesagake** right to left; enemy's cut slips off your shinogi as you draw.

No fixed follow-up attacks or noto method.

Simplest of the twelve in structure; foundational kesagake principle.
