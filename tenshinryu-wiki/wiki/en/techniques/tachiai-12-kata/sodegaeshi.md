# 6 — Sodegaeshi (袖返)
![Seiho 6: Sodegaeshi 袖返](/assets/tachiai-12-kata/sodegaeshi/%E6%8A%9C%E8%A8%8E/DSC_0267.JPG)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hidden name** | Nowakedachi 野分太刀 |
| **Type** | Self-defense + assassination (抜討) |

## Two branches

### A — Self-defense (護身)

From behind: enemy grabs **scabbard tip** (left) and **right sleeve** (right). Cannot draw normally.

- Move sword large up/down/left/right to weaken grip
- **In-yo turn**, scabbard flip draw, counter kesahazushi or kesagake
- **Chiburui:** Tenkachiburui | **Noto:** Sakatenoto (fixed)

### B — Assassination (抜討)

Follow target on narrow path; pass quickly; when guard drops — draw, turn left, cut up through jaw, cut down on head.

Finish: Kumoi → Sekiun → Seigan; Tenkachiburui / Sakatenoto.

**Nowakedachi:** whirl like autumn **野分** typhoon.
