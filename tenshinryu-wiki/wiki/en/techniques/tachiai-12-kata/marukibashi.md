# 9 — Marukibashi (丸木橋)

![Seiho 9: Marukibashi 丸木橋](/assets/tachiai-12-kata/marukibashi/%E4%B8%B8%E6%9C%A8%E6%A9%8B.jpg)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hidden names** | Inyoisso 陰陽一入, Hazamagaeshi 狭間返 |
| **Type** | Narrow suspension bridge — enemies front and rear |

## Situation

Crossing a **log suspension bridge** (or any unstable footing where lateral swings are impossible). Both enemies already drawn.

**Yagyugoshi** facing both. Right enemy cuts straight down → turn right, **Suribadachi**, flip tip, cut forearm. Left enemy cuts → turn left, blade down/mine up receive from below, cut forearm.

**Katagakenokurai** → slow right turn → exhale → **Seigannokurai**. Hold breath from start. Tenkachiburui / Sakatenoto.

## Footwork stages

1. Shumokuashi defend → step front foot to counter
2. Shumokuashi defend and counter in shumokuashi
3. Feet fixed on defend → shumokuashi on counter
