# 11 — Karamegaeshi (絡返)
![Seiho 11: Karamegaeshi 絡返](/assets/tachiai-12-kata/karamegaeshi/%E5%88%A5%E3%82%A2%E3%83%B3%E3%82%B0%E3%83%AB.jpg)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hidden names** | Fudokaramedachi 不動絡太刀, Koshiguruma 腰車 |
| **Type** | One-hand horizontal cut |

## Situation

Standing/walking; enemy sudden one-hand **horizontal** cut.

## Movement

1. Quick draw; tip up (**Fudoken** 不動剣); left foot diagonal back; block on shinogi; both feet **Shumokuashi**
2. Entangle — rotate sword right
3. Pull pommel to chest center; left hand on pommel
4. Right leg forward; push pommel — cut/stab right arm and right armpit; extreme forward lean
5. Follow with left foot; **Makurigiri** to forehead
6. Kumoi → Sekiun → Seigan → noto

**Koshiguruma:** return sword at waist like a wheel to kill enemy's blade.
