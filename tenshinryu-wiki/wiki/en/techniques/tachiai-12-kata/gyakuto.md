# 8 — Gyakuto (逆刀)
![Seiho 8: Gyakuto 逆刀](/assets/tachiai-12-kata/gyakuto/%E9%80%86%E5%88%80.jpg)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hidden names** | Tsukaotoshimogaridachi 柄下虎落太刀, Gyakubatsugaeshi 逆刃柄返し |
| **Type** | Close range — enemy grabs your hilt |

## Version A — Prevent grab

Left hand on scabbard; right **sakate** (reverse grip) on tsuka. Hug sword to right shoulder; oblique rear-right; **Shumokuashi**. Drop scabbard, high draw; switch to **junte** overhead; pull left leg back; **kesagake** right to left.

Usually **Kumoinokurai** (not Ogesan) → Sekiun → Seigan. Tenkachiburui / Sakatenoto.

## Version B — Hilt already grabbed

Work tsuka up/down/left/right/twist to break grip — then same as A.

**Tsukaotoshimogaridachi:** fierce downward cut like **虎落** wind on a fence.
