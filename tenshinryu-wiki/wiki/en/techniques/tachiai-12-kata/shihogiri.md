# 7 — Shihogiri (四方切)
![Seiho 7: Shihogiri 四方切](/assets/tachiai-12-kata/shihogiri/%E5%88%A5%E3%82%A2%E3%83%B3%E3%82%B0%E3%83%AB.jpg)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hidden names** | Shigaramidachi 柵太刀, Kesamidare 袈裟乱れ |
| **Type** | Four enemies — front, back, left, right |

## Setup

Enemies front/left/right drawn (jodan); **rear enemy not yet drawn**. You: **Yagyugoshi**, hilt raised, advance slowly.

## Four actions (order)

1. **Rear (1):** As he reaches for tsuka — twist clockwise, draw, kesagake right forearm
2. **Front (2):** **Suribadachi** scrape-deflect; kesagake left shoulder; extreme forward lean, tip near floor
3. **Right (3):** Before his cut — pull right leg wide, cut chest to right armpit
4. **Left (4):** Low leap on left axis; hikigiri left shoulder/arm; land right foot forward

**Katagakenokurai** (left shoulder) → slow clockwise turn on **right foot** → exhale → **Seigannokurai**. **Hold breath** from start until then. Tenkachiburui / Sakatenoto.

Mindset: expose opening to **rear enemy** to control timing (JP text).
