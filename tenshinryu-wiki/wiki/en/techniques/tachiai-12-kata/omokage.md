# 1 — Omokage (面陰)
![Seiho 1: Omokage 面陰](/assets/tachiai-12-kata/omokage/%E9%9D%A2%E9%99%B0.jpg)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hidden name** | Sansetsuken 傘雪剣 |
| **Type** | Boto (防刀) — defense against sudden cut |

## Situation

Standing or walking; opponent suddenly draws and cuts straight at the head (or kesagake).

## Movement (summary)

1. Draw while stepping slightly **diagonal forward-right**
2. Raise tip; right forearm near elbow to **right forehead** — receive on **shinogi/mine** (umbrella-in-snow imagery → *Sansetsuken*)
3. Turn tip; **kesa cut** between deltoid and biceps
4. Retreat ≥180 cm in **Ogesanokurai** or **Seigannokurai** → zanshin → noto (method free)

## JP text adds

- Twist upper body right on receive, left on return cut
- Targets may vary: collarbone, temple
- **Jutka:** 雪の道 傘に積りし…
