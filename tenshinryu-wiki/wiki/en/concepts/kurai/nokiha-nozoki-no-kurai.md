# Nokiha Nozoki-no-Kurai 軒端覗の位

日本語: [../../ja/concepts/kurai/nokiha-nozoki-no-kurai.md](../../ja/concepts/kurai/nokiha-nozoki-no-kurai.md)

**Also called:** Nokiha family

**Nozoki (覗)** — peeking from under eaves.

- Blade upward; kissaki at enemy; fist center at chest **Nakazumi**
- In: right forearm + sword straight line; Yo: left forearm + sword
- **Hanmi** + **Chojiashi**
