# Ten-no-Kurai (天の位)

**Jodan (上段)** — fist/sword held above the chest. Used heavily in [[techniques/tachiai-12-kata]] finishes.

| Kurai | Aliases | Role (summary) |
|-------|---------|----------------|
| **Kumoi-no-Kurai** 雲居 | Kenjodan, Tenjodan, Raito | Sword high piercing sky; fastest strike; **batto** after vertical cut + ≥6 shaku retreat |
| **Sekiun-no-Kurai** 切雲 | Setsuun, Raitokuzushi | Sideways tilt from Kumoi; offensive; **Hanmi** / Sosyadachi |
| **Hasso-no-Kurai** 八草 | Kesa-no-Kurai | Tall grass; kesagake; rush attacks; phone-hold elbows |
| **Ogesa-no-Kurai** 大袈裟 | Syadachi | Large Hasso; armor when helmet blocks Hasso; **after kesagake retreat** |
| **Mangetsu-no-Kurai** 満月 | — | Jodan; show tsukagashira like full moon, hide blade |
| **Toriikuzushi-no-Kurai** 鳥居崩 | (from Torii-no-Kurai) | Shrine gate shape broken; defense → instant attack |
| **Yokobue-no-Tachi** 横笛 (Ten) | — | Horizontal sword like flute; **Shimpo** / backlight / Hikari-no-Koto |
| **Tate-no-Kurai** 楯 (Ten) | Tatedachi, Ippo-no-Kurai | Shield; mountain peak; invite elbow cuts |
| **Jyatai-no-Kurai** 蛇躰 (Ten) | — | Snake head; chiburui; unusual — surprises enemy |
| **Nokihagakure-no-Kurai** 軒端隠 | Kasumi-no-kamae | Hide under eaves; rain/sweat/sun tactics (**Nokiha** family) |
