# Source summary — Guidelines for Teaching Beginners

**Author:** Kuwami Masakumo, 10th Shike (PDF: “Masakumo Kuwakai”)  
**Raw PDF:** `raw/books/TSR Books/Guidelines for Teaching Beginners.pdf` (7 pages)  
**Extracted text:** `raw/books/TSR Books/Guidelines for Teaching Beginners.txt`

## Key takeaways

1. Mixed-level group class needs an explicit strategy — never ignore beginners
2. Three group tactics: pre-brief beginners, assistant coaches, or adapted mimicry
3. Individual teaching: context first, avoid overload, calibrate expectations
4. Enjoyment + discipline — instructors serve students and keep learning
