# Source summary — On the Spirit of Chūgi (忠義)

**Author:** [[people/kuwami-masakumo|Kuwami Masakumo]], 10th Shike  
**Venue:** [[community/tenshinryu-hyoho-facebook|Tenshinryu Hyoho Official Community]] (Facebook, private)  
**Posted:** 2026-06-26  
**Raw archive:** `raw/community/kuwami-spirit-of-chugi-2025-06.md`

## Key takeaways

1. **Chūgi** (忠義) = devoted service (忠) aligned with righteousness (義); central Confucian virtue shaping samurai duty
2. Tenshinryu techniques gain depth only when understood against this mission — not as sport or surface form
3. **Hagakure** (葉隠) Book 1 sections 16–137 (excerpted): lord-first ethics, self-blame, hōkō (奉公), messhi hōkō (滅私奉公)
4. Ideal is almost "mad" loyalty — rare in practice, but upheld as standard; links to **onko chishin** (温故知新)
5. Full Hagakure kakikudashi text: [sagalibdb.jp/hagakure/list02](https://www.sagalibdb.jp/hagakure/list02)
