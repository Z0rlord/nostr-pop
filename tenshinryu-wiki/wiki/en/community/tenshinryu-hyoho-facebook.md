# Tenshinryu Hyoho Official Community (Facebook)

**Tenshinryu Hyoho Official Community** is a **private** Facebook group for members of the Tenshinryu Hyoho federation and **Tenshinryu ONLINE** program (~2.5K members as of 2026). Posts require **admin approval** to join.

## Purpose

A members-only forum where instructors and senior practitioners share essays, announcements, and discussion — not a public substitute for live instruction or the official websites.

## Practice log (brief)

The same feed may include member practice posts (e.g. daily keiko shorts tagged `#dojopop` on YouTube). These are personal training logs, not hyoho curriculum — see [dojopop.live](https://dojopop.live) for the Nostr-native practice platform.

## Access

Membership is by invitation or request through official Tenshinryu channels. This page is a **pointer for existing members**; it does not reproduce private group content beyond ingested lessons cited above.
