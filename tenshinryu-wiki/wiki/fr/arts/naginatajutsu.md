**Naginatajutsu (薙刀術)** — Résumé provisoire : The art of the glaive (*naginata*) — a curved blade on a long shaft. One of eight major arts in Tenshinryu hyoho (see arts/_index), grouped with other long weapons under Hozoin-line spear tradition.
