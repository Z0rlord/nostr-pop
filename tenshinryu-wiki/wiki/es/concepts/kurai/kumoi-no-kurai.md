# Kumoi-no-Kurai 雲居の位

**También llamado:**** Kenjodan · Tenjodan · Raito 雷刀
**Kumoi (雲居)** — " dwelling in the clouds / far high sky." Sword raised as if to pierce heaven with the kissaki.

## Cuerpo

- Codo derecho **extended**; fists and sword at **Nakazumi (中墨)**
- Sword tilted slightly **back**
- Left fist must **not** pass forehead — forehead and little-finger side of left fist in one line
- Back foot: **Kagiashi**

## Función

- Ataque; kurai de espera for **Sasoibara** (誘い腹 — invite cut to abdomen)
- Strike from this kurai se considera el **más rápido**

## Variaciones

- Left or right foot forward; **Shumokuashi**; **Tendachi** — all **Taimen (対面 / Tosyadachi 刀者立)**

## Battojutsu

- Tras un **corte vertical directo**, retroceder **≥6 shaku (180 cm+)** into Kumoi.
