# Chi-no-Kurai (地の位)

**Gedan (下段)** — fist below chest. Waiting, inviting, and low-line defense.
| Kurai | Role |
|-------|------|
| **Suigetsu-no-Kurai** 水月 | Moon on water; waiting at solar plexus; wheel imagery (Yokoguruma, Kurumadachi); **Minetani / Hokoku** exposes shoulder valley |
| **Jisuri-no-Seigan** 地摺の青眼 | Tip down dragging ground; invites attack; saves energy in long fights |
| **Chi-no-Kurai-Kuzushi** 地の位崩し | Tip left/right off line from Jisuri; easier to bait |
| **Tsuka Otoshi-mune-no-To** 柄落胸刀 | Hilt dropped; mine on shoulder; armor & suhada — minimal target |
| **Nokihagake-no-Kurai** 軒端掛 | Sitting on eaves edge; thumb/index grip; deep invite |
| **Yokobue-no-Tachi** (Chi) | Gedan waiting variant |
| **Tate-no-Kurai** (Chi) | Inverted shield; tip down; leverage draw |
Common pattern: **Hanmi** + **Nenoashi** or **Chojiashi**; forward lean; hide blade show tsukagashira.
