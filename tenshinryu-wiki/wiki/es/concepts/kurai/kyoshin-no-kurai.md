# Kyoshin-no-Kurai 虚身の位

Feign injury/disability:
- Hidden thumb; splint arm (breakaway branch); pebble in tabi; carp-scale "blindness"; deaf-mute act; battle fatigue

## Jutka

> 虚躰の位で立ち相う誘い太刀
