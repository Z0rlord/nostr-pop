# Yokobue-no-Tachi (Shin) 横笛太刀・真

Shin-tier Yokobue — body slightly forward; tip lowered. Waiting kurai — enemy aims at fists/head.
- **Hanmi**; **Chojiashi** to **Kagiashi**
- Defends against Ten, Shin, and Chi line attacks
