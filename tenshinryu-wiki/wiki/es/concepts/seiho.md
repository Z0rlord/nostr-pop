# Seiho (勢法)

## Resumen

 *(Resumen automático del texto fuente; consulte la versión inglesa para detalle.)*

In Tenshinryu, **勢法** (*seiho*, read *kata*) denotes **special, advanced techniques** — not the ordinary 型/形 (*kata*) used in general budo terminology. The character choice marks a separate category of transmission tied to combat philosophy.

Within **立相抜刀術** (*tachiai battojutsu* — standing draw-and-cut), there are exactly **twelve seiho**. They condense the essence of the ryu; Tenshin Sensei historically introduced them early so students contact core teachings sooner.
