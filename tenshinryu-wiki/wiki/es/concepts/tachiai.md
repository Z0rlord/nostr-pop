# Tachiai (立相 / 立合)

## Resumen

 *(Resumen automático del texto fuente; consulte la versión inglesa para detalle.)*

**Tachiai** in this curriculum means **standing confrontation** — both parties facing each other for a duel — and the draw-and-cut methods used in that context.

The twelve seiho of [[techniques/tachiai-12-kata]] are the advanced standing battojutsu syllabus. The Japanese title uses 立相; English materials often write 立合 — same teaching line.
