# Nakamura Tenshin (中村天心)

**9th Shike** of Tenshinryu Hyoho. **Tenshin (天心)** is martial arts **bugo** (武号) from Ishii Seizo.

## Biografía (Miden Kurai-no-Koto)

- Nacido en Tokio **1943**
- Infancia: judo, karate, iaido, jujutsu; fascinated by **nihonzashi** (two swords)
- Met **8th Shike Ishii Seizo**; endured severe training when other students quit
- Abrió **Hyoho Juku** at home per Ishii's wish; **musha shugyo** street performances nationwide
- Wife died young; taught mainly relatives/acquaintances while raising three children
- After retirement, reopened public study
- **2012-02-11:** appointed **Kuwami Masakumo** as 10th Shike; continues overseeing instruction
