# 9 — Marukibashi (丸木橋)

## Resumen

 *(Resumen automático del texto fuente; consulte la versión inglesa para detalle.)*

Crossing a **log suspension bridge** (or any unstable footing where lateral swings are impossible). Both enemies already drawn.

**Yagyugoshi** facing both. Right enemy cuts straight down → turn right, **Suribadachi**, flip tip, cut forearm. Left enemy cuts → turn left, blade down/mine up receive from below, cut forearm.
