**Sanmi-no-Kurai — Three Standing Bodies (三躰の位)** — Vorläufige Zusammenfassung: Collective name for Tenshinryu's three basic ways of standing — also ChikuSyaRoboku (竹斜老木). With sword in hand: Sanmitachi-no-Kurai (三躰太刀の位).
