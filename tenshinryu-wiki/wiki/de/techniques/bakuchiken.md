**Bakuchiken (驀地剣)** — Vorläufige Zusammenfassung: Common name for Nukidome (抜留), seiho #3 of techniques/tachiai-12-kata. Pre-empt the opponent's draw by targeting the drawing hand; low draw hides intent below the partner's sword brim.
