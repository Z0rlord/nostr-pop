Tenshinryu Combat Maid 
In Japan, May 10th is Maid’s Day. 
A few years ago, he uploaded a video of himself performing in a costume invented by his mother, and it became a hot topic not only in Japan but also around the world. 
It is important to carry on the tradition correctly, but it is also important to be playful and promote it to the public. 
Kuwami Sensei always says. 
“If no one knows about it, it is the same as not existing.” 
Some people say. 
“The ancient martial arts don’t put out videos! 
We don’t advertise! 
Only those who really want to learn it should learn it!” 
It’s one of their beliefs. We do not deny it. 
However, for decades, Tenshinryu was almost unknown and on the verge of being lost with very few students, limited training opportunities, number of training sessions, and training time. 
This is one strategy for communicating the right things correctly.
