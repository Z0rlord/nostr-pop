“Takizawa Dofu using Goto(A long and heavy sword)” 
The length of the blade is about 81 cm(二尺七寸). 
In TENSHINRYU, the sword used in actual battle is about 66 cm(二尺二寸). 
This is shorter than usual. 
It is the optimum length when considering indoor battle, a confused fight and portability etc. 
However, it is important to use long swords in practice. 
It is not just practice of drawing Sword. 
By knowing the advantages and disadvantages of a long and heavy sword, it will be advantageous when fighting with it.
